TERMS AND CONDITIONS OF MYID ELOFUN PROMOTION

  1. Name of the promotion: MyID Elofun Championship Leaderboard
  2. Frequency: Weekly
  3. Duration: Every week there will be a Championship starting from 12:00:00PM Sunday to 11:59:59AM next Sunday.
  4. Participant: All Mytel subscribers who use MyID application can play the various games.
  5. Mechanism: For each winning match in PvP or Tournament mode, user will accumulate certain number of Championship point (CP).
  • CP earn rate: CP = total gaining point = total of coin rewarded amount.
  • Top 10 highest ranking of championship point in the weekly leaderboard at the time of closure (11:59:59 AM next Sunday) will get the prizes as per prize structure in Mytel Emoney.
  • User can check and see the CP points of himself or herself and the other user in the leaderboard menu which is accessible in the banner on top of MyID Elofun homepage menu.
  • When the leaderboard ends, all users’CP are reset to be 0 to start a new weekly leaderboard contest.

  1. Rule of winner
  • Top 10 highest point of championship board will get the prizes via Mytelpay as per the prize structure. Winners should be claiming their rewards in Mytelpay.
  • If more than one player has the same championship points, the prize will be awarded to the player with more coins left in the account balance at the time of leaderboard closure.
  • MyID Elofun will inform the winners via SMS or inside the game as soon as possible but no later than 1 day (24hrs) from the date of leaderboard closure.
  • After customers receive the reward notification, they need to claim the Mytelpay prizes within 8 days since the end-date of the weekly leaderboard. if they do not claim the prize within 8 days, the prizes will automatically be expired and disrupted.
  • Mytelpay account of the winner has to be the same as the phone number which your MyID account phone number won the prize.
  • If Customers have Mytelpay account already: just claim prize on MyID Elofun game, they will get the prize to their Mytelpay account. If customers haven’t Mytelpay account, they have to create account before get the prizes.
  • In case of a dispute or claim of a prize, the decision of Mytel MyID is final.
  • Players might need to provide ID card (if required) for Mytel’s record as proof of prize delivery.
